NWCG GeoOps Wildfire Iconset — TAK Reference Sheet

Version: R2 (August 2025) Standard: NWCG PMS 936 (2025) Tested: ATAK 5.4.0.16 & WinTAK
Community-maintained iconset for ATAK/WinTAK aligned with the 2025 NWCG GeoOps symbology standards (PMS 936). Icons are organized into groups as they appear in the ATAK icon palette. New icons added in R2 are noted in the README. Feedback and corrections welcome — see the repository for contact info.
